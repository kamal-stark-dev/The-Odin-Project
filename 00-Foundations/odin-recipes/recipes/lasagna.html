Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna (or lasagne) is a classic Italian dish consisting of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings like ragù (meat and tomato sauce), béchamel or ricotta cheese, and mozzarella or Parmesan, then baked. Originating in Naples, it is a quintessential Italian comfort food commonly served as a hearty main course.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce (Ragù, Béchamel)
  • Cheeses (Ricotta, Mozzarella, Parmesan)
  • Sesoning (Basil, Parsley, Oregano)
